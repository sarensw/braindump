Logs are stored here:
on Linux: ~/.config/{app name}/logs/{process type}.log
on macOS: ~/Library/Logs/{app name}/{process type}.log
on Windows: %USERPROFILE%\AppData\Roaming\{app name}\logs\{process type}.log
